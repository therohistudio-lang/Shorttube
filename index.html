About ShortTube

What is ShortTube?

ShortTube is a mobile media-sharing app with a photo feed and a YouTube-style long-form video feed, mixing your own community's uploads together with public videos from YouTube.

Why we built it

To give independent creators a simple place to publish both quick clips and longer videos side-by-side with the wider video world, without needing their own audience first.

Version

ShortTube Web — built with Appwrite (auth, database, storage) and vanilla JS.
